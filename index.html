About

This project demonstrates the design and evaluation of a Hybrid RPA-AI Framework for automating transcript processing in higher education. It integrates Robotic Process Automation (RPA) with Artificial Intelligence (AI) modules such as OCR and NLP for faster, more accurate, and privacy-compliant academic workflows.

Projects & Methodology

  • 📊 Dataset analysis and preprocessing
  • 🤖 RPA (UiPath) for automation of structured tasks
  • 🧠 AI Modules (OCR + NLP) for intelligent data extraction and validation
  • 🔐 Compliance layer ensuring FERPA/GDPR adherence

Results & Outcomes

  • ⚡ Transcript processing time reduced by 70%
  • ✅ Error rate reduced by 85%
  • 📈 Daily throughput increased by 233%
  • 🔐 Full compliance with FERPA/GDPR standards
